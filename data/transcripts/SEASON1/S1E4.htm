[Nibbler eats the Hermaphlamingos, making lots of disgusting noises. The crew watch and cringe.]

(16:43) ⨂Leela: Nibbler!

(16:44) ⨂Fry: I can't believe we flew halfway across the galaxy and enjoyed a steam just to get lunch for that stupid animal!

(16:50) ⨂Bender: He's pending for a bending.

[He rolls up his sleeves and chases Nibbler. He runs to Leela and she picks him up.]

(16:54) ⨂Leela: Leave him alone. It's not his fault that he's an unstoppable killing machine. Is it, snoogums?

[The ground shakes, rocking the ship.]

(17:01) ⨂Fry: The planet's kerploding!

[Cut to: Vergon 6 Surface. Fry runs out through a door and falls through the ground. Bender reaches into the hole and pulls him back up.]
[Cut to: Ships Cockpit.]

(17:09) ⨂Leela: Prepare for lift-off. [She turns the key in the ignition but the engine doesn't start. The fuel gauge reads empty.] We're out of fuel. Bender, I told you to fill the tank before we left.

(17:17) ⨂Bender: Yeah, I'll do it when we get back.

[The ground shakes again.]

(17:21) ⨂Fry: Man, lucky for us Zapp Brannigan's nearby.

(17:24) ⨂Leela: No way! Forget it! I refuse to go crawling back to him!

(17:27) ⨂Fry: What? What are you talking about?

(17:29) ⨂Leela: [defensively] Nothing. We just talked.

(17:31) ⨂Bender: So what's your problem? It's not like you slept with him. [Leela hangs her head in shame.] Oh, my God!

(17:42) ⨂Fry: How could you, Leela? I thought you had some standards. I mean, jeez, he's a dumb, gross gorilla!

(17:47) ⨂Leela: That's enough! Don't you think I feel bad enough already?

(17:51) ⨂Fry: No!

[The ground shakes violently.]

(17:53) ⨂Leela: Alright, alright. I'll call him. [She presses a button and the videophone comes down.] I mean, if living is that important to you.

[Scene: Nimbus Bridge.]

(17:59) ⨂Zapp: And that's why you'll never make captain, Kif. ["Incoming Message" repeatedly flashes on the viewscreen.] Hmm.

[Leela face appears.]

(18:04) ⨂Leela: [on screen] Hello, Zapp?

(18:05) ⨂Zapp: Well, well, well!

[Cut to: Ships Cockpit.]

(18:08) ⨂Leela: Zapp, we're out of fuel...

[Cut to: Nimbus Bridge.]

(18:10) ⨂Leela: [on screen] ...and Vergon 6 is about to implode. We need your help.

(18:12) ⨂Zapp: So, crawling back to the Big Z like a bird on its belly. Delicious.

(18:18) ⨂Leela: [on screen] Bird's don't crawl.

(18:19) ⨂Zapp: They've been known to.

[Cut to: Ships Cockpit.]

(18:21) ⨂Leela: Look, are you going to rescue us or not?

(18:23) ⨂Zapp: [on screen] Can't you ask a little more ... sexfully?

[Fry and Bender make encouragig gestures at Leela.]

(18:29) ⨂Leela: [sexfully] Please?

[Cut to: Nimbus Bridge.]

(18:30) ⨂Leela: [sexfully; on screen] Big Z?

(18:32) ⨂Zapp: Certainly. But first you'll have to get rid of that thing. [He points at Nibbler] That's the law, Leela. And Brannigan's Law is like Brannigan's love: Hard and fast!

[Cut to: Ships Cockpit.]

(18:42) ⨂Zapp: [on screen] Now put that greasy rat outside and we'll tow you to safety.

(18:45) ⨂Leela: I would never abandon a helpless animal. Y'know, Zapp, once I thought you were a big, pompous buffoon. Then I realised that inside you were just a pitiful child. But now I realise that outside that child is a big, pompous buffoon!

(18:59) ⨂Zapp: [on screen] And which one rocked your world?

[Leela growls and slams down the receiver and his face fizzles off the screen.]

(19:03) ⨂Fry: Wow! Way to tell that guy off. Now what's your secret escape plan?

(19:07) ⨂Leela: Uh, I guess to sit here and wait for death.

(19:10) ⨂Bender: Can do!

[He puts his feet up and reclines in his chair. There is a huge bang and the ship jerks backwards.]

(19:13) ⨂Bender: What the hell was that?

[Scene: Ships Cargo Bay. There is a big, round, black blob in Nibbler's litter tray.]

(19:18) ⨂Fry: Pew!

[He fans the air in front of his nose.]

(19:20) ⨂Leela: I don't believe it! It's dark matter.

(19:22) ⨂Bender: So this guy just unloaded a steaming pile of starship fuel?

(19:26) ⨂Leela: His species must've filled the entire planet with it. Did you do that, you cute little-- [The ship shakes again.] Come on. That should be more than enough fuel to get us outta here. Bender, pick it up and put it in the engine.

[Bender mutters to himself and drags the dark matter across the floor towards the engine room.]
[Cut to: Ships Engine Room. Bender throws the dark matter into the furnace.]
[Cut to: Ships Cockpit. The fuel gauge fills up, Leela throttles forward and the ship lifts off, moments before the ground collapses. They fly away from the planet and it explodes. The crew cheer.]

(20:04) ⨂Leela: We made it! [She looks through a pair of binoculars.] And some of the animals survived.

[She looks through the binoculars and sees some animals standing on a tiny fragment of rock.]

(20:09) ⨂Bender: So a couple of animals didn't die and Leela got lucky. That's what I call a successful mission!

(20:15) ⨂Fry: We're heroes!

[Scene: Leela's Quarters. Leela writes in her diary. Nibbler lies on her lap and she strokes him.]

(20:18) ⨂Leela: [thinking] Dear Captain's Diary: I may not have found love on this mission but I did find a cute little companion who excretes starship fuel. And that's just as good.

[She pauses for a moment then tears the page out, screws it up and throws it in the bin.]
[Scene: Outside Nimbus.]

(20:32) ⨂Zapp: [from inside] Captain's journal: Star date ... uh.

[Kif sighs.]

(20:35) ⨂Kif: [from inside] April 13th.

(20:37) ⨂Zapp: [from inside] April 13th ... point two.

[Cut to: Nimbus Bridge.]

(20:40) ⨂Zapp: We have failed to uphold Brannigan's Law. However, I did make it with a hot alien babe. And in the end is that not what man has dreamt of since first he looked up at the stars? Kif, I'm asking you a question!

[Kif sighs.]
[Closing Credits.]
